About Minecraft

What is Minecraft?

Minecraft is a sandbox game where players explore a blocky, procedurally generated 3D world with infinite terrain. Players can discover and extract raw materials, craft tools and items, and build structures or earthworks.

History

Created by Markus "Notch" Persson and developed by Mojang Studios, Minecraft was first released in 2011. It became the best-selling video game of all time, with over 238 million copies sold across platforms.

Game Editions

Minecraft is available in several editions including Java Edition (PC), Bedrock Edition (consoles and mobile), Education Edition, and China-exclusive versions. Each offers unique features while maintaining the core gameplay.

Game Modes

Survival

Gather resources, craft tools, build shelters, and fight mobs to survive in this challenging mode where health and hunger matter.

Creative

Unlimited resources and the ability to fly let you focus purely on building without restrictions or threats.

Adventure

Play custom maps created by other players with special rules and objectives designed for unique experiences.

Spectator

Observe worlds without interacting, flying through blocks to watch others play from any perspective.

Iconic Blocks

Meet the Mobs

Creeper

Stealthy hostile mob that silently approaches players before exploding.

Skeleton

Undead archer that shoots arrows at players from a distance.

Enderman

Neutral mob that teleports when looked at directly in the eyes.

Spider

Hostile mob that climbs walls and attacks at night.

Sheep

Passive mob that provides wool which can be dyed different colors.

Cow

Passive mob that provides leather and beef when slaughtered.

Zombie Pigman

Neutral Nether mob that attacks when provoked.

Slime

Bouncy cube mob that splits into smaller slimes when killed.
